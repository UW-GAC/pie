Find a dbGaP accessioned object

dbGaP accession identifiers (IDs) are assigned by dbGaP to studies, sub-studies and their subordinate digital objects. These IDs are dbGaP-unique, stable and versioned identifiers. They include both a identifier and a version. All accession ids are unique across dbGaP.

PIE stores information for three different types of accession:

  • Studies (example: phs000007)
  • Datasets (example: pht000009)
  • Variables (example: phv00000507)

    • This page allows you to find these three types of objects on PIE using either the accession id or the name of the study, dataset, or variable in dbGaP.
