Phenotype Inventory Explorer

PIE allows TOPMed investigators to view and search through the phenotype data currently available to the TOPMed Data Coordinating Center. The DCC is in the process of combining study phenotypes across TOPMed projects to produce harmonized phenotypes. TOPMed Working Groups can help the harmonization process by submitting details for how to combine study phenotypes.

Study phenotypes

Phenotype data from each study is downloaded from dbGaP and stored securely at the TOPMed DCC. Metadata (but not phenotype data values) are scanned and stored by PIE to facilitate browsing, searching, and tagging phenotypic variables and datasets. dbGaP accession numbers uniquely identify each study, dataset, and variable. These study variables are used as the source material for the DCC's reproducible and rigorously documented phenotype harmonization process.

Harmonized phenotypes

The TOPMed DCC is producing a high quality, lasting resource of publicly available and thoroughly documented harmonized phenotype variables. The DCC works with Working Group members and study and phenotype experts to define a target harmonized variable and idenfity the component study variables to use. Harmonized variables are listed on PIE as they become available. Once finalized, datasets containing harmonized variables can be downloaded from the dbGaP TOPMed exchange area by authorized TOPMed investigators (more information here). Eventually these harmonized phenotypes will be available from dbGaP.
